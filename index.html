BoxBoxBox

BoxBoxBox is a work-in-progress motorsport management game that focuses on emergent-narrative.

The game features an accurate physics simulation of how real vehicles works, while the driving AI is based on context steering behaviours and can be fine-tuned in order to simulate different types of drivers and racing styles.

Thanks to its smart AI and a rich cast of drivers with different skills and abilities, the game can endlessly generate engaging and unpredictable races.

OVERHEAT

OVERHEAT was my entry for the Blackthornprod GAME JAM #3, a one-week Game Jam with over one thousand entries.

The theme of the jam was 'Less is More', so the game gets more difficult by giving you less power ups every time you beat it.

The game finished in the top 20% of the entries despite being made only in the last three days of the jam.

Bolganone

Bolganone is a companion app for Fire Emblem Heroes, a popular gacha game for mobile platforms.

Thanks to its features and completeness, the app quickly surpassed other competitors and became the most popular FEH companion app on Android, reaching 12k users and a rating of 4.8 stars.

Bolganone was also featured in different videos by a popular content creator of the community.

LoR Wiki

LoR Wiki was the first companion app for Legends of Runeterra to land on Android. It has over 5k downloads and a 4+ stars rating.

The app features a database with all the cards currently avaiable in the game, a deck builder and a match simulator.

Lor Wiki was selected for the Legends of Runeterra Developer Challenge held by Riot Games.

D3EFunk - The Game

MadD3E and Pole, a Music Producer and a Game Developer, teamed up to bring their dream to reality.

D3EFunk - The Game is a shoot'em up game heavily driven by music. It takes inspiration from shumps classics like DoDonPachi and Cho Ren Sha 68k, mixing them with rhythm games like OSU.

The soundtrack is taken from the "D3Efunk Vol. 3" by MadD3E, a futuristic EP that mixes funk and electronic using playful arrangements.

Remini

Remini uses innovative, state-of-the-art AI technology to transform your old photos into HD masterpieces.

It also allows to generate images using only your selfies: do you want to see yourself as a medieval king or a manga character? Remini can do that for you.

In August 2024, Remini surpassed ChatGPT as the most downloaded AI app.
